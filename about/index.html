About

I'm a first-year graduate student researcher at Scripps Institution for Oceanography at UCSD. I have graduated with a B.S in Applied Mathematics from UNC Chapel Hill, where I conducted fluid dynamics research in the Joint Applied Math and Marine Sciences Fluids Lab under the guidance of Professor Rich McLaughlin and Professor Roberto Camassa. Over the past two years, I have also conducted fluid dynamics research in Professor Shilpa Khatri's lab in the Applied Mathematics Group at UC Merced.
